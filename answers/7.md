## 2. So sánh Camunda 7 vs Camunda 8

| Feature | Platform 7 (Free) | Platform 8 (Enterprise) |
|---------|-------------------|------------------------|
| **Engine** | Java-based | Zeebe (Go-based, cloud-native) |
| **Database** | Relational (MySQL, PostgreSQL) | Columnar (Elasticsearch/Rockset) |
| **Scalability** | Vertical (scale up) | Horizontal (scale out) |
| **Deployment** | Monolith/Microservices | Microservices only |
| **License** | Apache 2.0 (free) | Commercial |
| **Performance** | ~100-500 instances/sec | ~1000+ instances/sec |
| **Multi-tenancy** | ❌ | ✅ |
| **User Management** | Basic | Advanced |

## 8. Kết luận

### Camunda Free vs Paid:

| Yếu tố | Free (Platform 7) | Paid (Platform 8/Cloud) |
|--------|-------------------|------------------------|
| **License** | Apache 2.0 | Commercial |
| **Cost** | $0 | $10,000+/năm |
| **Features** | Basic BPMN | Full enterprise |
| **Support** | Community | 24/7 Enterprise |
| **Scale** | Medium | High |

### Top Free Alternatives:

1. **Camunda 7** - Best overall free BPMN
2. **Flowable** - Lightweight Activiti fork
3. **Temporal** - Best for developers (code-first)
4. **jBPM** - Best for rule-based workflows
5. **Activiti** - Simple, lightweight

### Top Paid Alternatives:

1. **Camunda 8** - Best balance
2. **Appian** - Best low-code
3. **Pega** - Best for large enterprises
4. **IBM BAW** - Best for IBM shops
