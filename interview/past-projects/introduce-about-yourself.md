# Tell me a little bit about yourself.

========== THIS SECTION IS THE ANSWER ==========

I have over 5 years of experience in software development, primarily as a Java backend developer using Spring Boot. Most of my career — about 5 years — was spent working on one of the largest automotive marketplaces in the US, serving around 30 million users per month. I was responsible for the Vehicle Search Results Page on both frontend and backend, working with a microservices architecture on AWS across two regions.

In that role, I led several key initiatives: breaking the monolith into microservices using the Strangler Fig pattern, building a natural language search feature with AWS Bedrock, optimizing SEO and Web Vitals, and contributing to a new accessible UI library. The tech stack included Spring Boot, Apache Solr, PostgreSQL, DynamoDB, Next.js, and heavy AWS integration — Lambda, SQS, SNS, ElastiCache, CloudFront.

Currently, I'm working on an ecommerce platform for a Korean client — selling furniture and interior design services. The interesting part is an AI-powered product visualization feature where users upload their room photo and the system generates images showing how our products would look in their space. I'm handling full-stack development with Spring Boot, Next.js, and MariaDB.

I'm looking for my next challenge as a senior backend developer where I can apply my experience with large-scale systems, microservices, and cloud-native architecture.
